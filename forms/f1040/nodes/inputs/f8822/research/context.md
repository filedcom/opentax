# Form 8822 — Change of Address

## Purpose
Form 8822 is used to notify the IRS of a change in home mailing address. It can also be used to change the address for a spouse filing separately.

## Key Fields
- **Line 1**: Check if change of home address
- **Lines 3-5**: Your name, SSN, new address (street, city, state, ZIP, foreign country)
- **Lines 6-7**: Spouse's name and SSN (if applicable)
- **Prior address**: Old address for IRS matching

## Tax Treatment
Administrative form only. No tax computations. Purely informational to update IRS records.

## Drake KB Notes
- Screen code: 8822
- Distinct from Form 8822-B (business address change)
- All address fields except new_address are optional
- Foreign filers may omit state/ZIP and use country instead
