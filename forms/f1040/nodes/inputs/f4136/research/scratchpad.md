# Form 4136 Research Scratchpad

## Source: IRS Form 4136 Instructions (TY2025)
- Form title: Credit for Federal Tax Paid on Fuels
- IRC §§ 6421, 6427
- Filed with Form 1040; singleton (one form per return)
- Multiple "lines" covering different fuel types and use purposes

## TY2025 Credit Rates (IRS Notice 2025 / Publication 510)
Per IRS Pub 510 and Form 4136 instructions for TY2025:

### Gasoline (line 1a/1b)
- Off-highway business use: $0.184/gal → nonrefundable → Schedule 3 line 6z
- Farming use: $0.184/gal → REFUNDABLE → Form 1040 line 35

### Diesel (line 2a/2b)
- Off-highway business use: $0.244/gal → nonrefundable → Schedule 3 line 6z
- Farming use: $0.244/gal → REFUNDABLE → Form 1040 line 35

### Aviation Gasoline (line 3)
- Use other than in commercial aviation: $0.194/gal → nonrefundable → Schedule 3 line 6z
- Farming use: $0.194/gal → REFUNDABLE → Form 1040 line 35

### Kerosene (line 4a/4b/4c)
- Use other than in commercial aviation (line 4a): $0.244/gal → nonrefundable
- Kerosene for use in aviation (non-commercial, line 4b): $0.219/gal → nonrefundable
- Farming use kerosene: $0.244/gal → REFUNDABLE

### Liquefied Petroleum Gas / LPG (line 5)
- Off-highway business use: $0.183/gal → nonrefundable → Schedule 3 line 6z

### Compressed Natural Gas / CNG (line 6)
- Off-highway business use: $0.183/GGE (gasoline gallon equivalent) → nonrefundable

### Other Fuels (lines 7-14+)
- Various rates for: liquid fuel (inland waterway), diesel for buses, etc.

## Refundable vs. Nonrefundable
- REFUNDABLE: Fuel used on a farm for farming purposes (all fuel types)
- NONREFUNDABLE: Off-highway business use, aviation use (not farming)
- Refundable credit → Form 1040 line 35 (other refundable credits)
- Nonrefundable credit → Schedule 3 line 6z (general business credit)

## Drake Screen 4136
- Single screen "4136" with PAGE DOWN for additional entries
- Collects gallons per fuel type per use purpose
- Software computes credit amounts

## Notes
- Taxpayer must have records of gallons purchased and use purpose
- Fuel must be used in the US
- Aviation gasoline for commercial aviation: different rules (not covered here)
