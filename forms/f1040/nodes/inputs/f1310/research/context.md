# Form 1310 — Statement of Person Claiming Refund Due a Deceased Taxpayer

## Purpose
Form 1310 is filed by a person (other than a surviving spouse filing jointly or a personal representative) claiming a refund on behalf of a deceased taxpayer.

## Key Fields
- **Part I**: Claimant type — spouse filing jointly (A), court-appointed representative (B), or other (C)
- **Part II**: Refund information — deceased's name, SSN, date of death
- **Part III**: Claimant certification — proof of death, court certificate

## Claimant Types
- **A (SpouseFilingJointly)**: Surviving spouse who was filing a joint return
- **B (CourtAppointedRepresentative)**: Court-appointed or certified personal representative
- **C (Other)**: Any other person claiming the refund (requires proof of death)

## Tax Treatment
Administrative form only. No tax computations. Directs refund to proper claimant.
